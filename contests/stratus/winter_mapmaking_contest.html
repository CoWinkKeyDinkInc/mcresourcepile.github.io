    • Contest Information
    Dates and Times
    Submissions open January 31st, 2019
    Submissions close March 4th, 2019
    Public testing March 8th, 2019
    Results announced April 9th, 2019
    Scoring (Total scores are the sum of each criteria)
    Gameplay out of 40
    Aesthetics out of 30
    Innovation out of 20
    Additional Bonus of 3% for XML with minimal errors and proper conventions
    Additional Bonus of 3% for map gamemodes in high demand
    Additional Bonus of 2% for map gamemodes in medium demand
    Additional Penalty of -5 points for author didn't do something right
    Details and Guidelines
    Maps could be any gamemode, but must be between 4 v 4 to 20 v 20
    Maps must be in a team format, no FFA
    XML documents were to be included with the submission. XML must be at least protocol 1.4.0
    Authors were allowed to be author on up to 3 maps, but only their top scored map was counted
    Maps previously submitted to Stratus Network could not be entered into this contest
    Prizes
    First Strato, 5x Set Next tokens, winner trophy, winner tag on discord, guaranteed spot in Rotatation Update #20
    Second 3 weeks of Nimbus, 5x Set Next tokens, runner up tag on discord
    Third 3 weeks of Nimbus, 3x Set Next tokens

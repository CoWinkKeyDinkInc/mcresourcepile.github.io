Knockout Round
Before the Invitations, there was a knockout round. The knockout round were all scheduled matches, and they all took place on Sunday, August 21st, 2016. 6 of the lowest seeds of the 32 originally selected teams had to face 6 more chosen teams who were able to have enough to sign up. Maps were chosen beforehand for the knockout round with a Bo3 gamemodes (vetoing to determine order) with SSB, NextGen, Ascendance & Storm & Bliss TDM.

Results: Bold moved on to Invitations

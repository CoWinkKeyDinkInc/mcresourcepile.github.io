Tournament Information
Details
Method Single Elimination (Bo3)
Gamemodes Capture the Wool, Destroy the Core and Monument, Team Deathmatch
Teams 16 teams
Team Size 8v8
Dates
Tournament January 19, 2013
Map Selection
Each round consisted of:
  • 1 Capture the Wool style map (45 minute time limit)
  • 1 Team Deathmatch style map
  • 1 Destroy the Core / Destroy the Monument style map (30 minute time limit)
  • Blitz sudden death matches in the case of a tie (10 minute time limit)

Round 1, QuarterFinals, and SemiFinals each had predetermined maps and order.

Round 1:
  • Race for Victory 2
  • Harb
  • Airship Battles

QuarterFinals:
  • Golden Drought
  • Zoo: Tournament
  • Cake Wars

SemiFinals:
  • Race for Victory
  • Cairo TDM
  • Spaceship Battles

Finals: "For each match, both teams chose half of the available maps, as maps they "want to play." If 1 map overlaps, you play that map. If more than 1 overlaps, teams repeat the process (chose half of the available maps, and vote on the overlap). If there is an even number of maps to chose from the teams chose half of the maps plus 1. If there are two maps left and teams cannot agree on a map, a ref will chose the map."

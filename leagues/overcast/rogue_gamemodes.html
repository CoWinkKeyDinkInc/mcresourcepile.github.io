Map Selection

Knockout Round
Teams will play a match of War Wars: Redux and the winner will have the option to veto first or second of the 3 gamemodes to play 1 gamemode. After that the loser of the match will have the option to veto first or second for the maps of the gamemode until there is 1 map remaining (DTC/CTW) or 3 maps remaining (Conquest). In the case of a tie, the loser of the War Wars match will have the option to veto first or second of the 5 tie breaker maps to play 1 map.
